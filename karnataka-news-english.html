Attack on Israeli Citizen in Karnataka: Strict Action Ordered

A shocking incident of assault and rape involving an Israeli citizen and a homestay owner has emerged from a village in Karnataka, India. The incident has caused widespread concern across the state, with locals worried about safety. Karnataka CM Siddaramaiah has termed it a "heinous crime" and ordered immediate strict action against the perpetrators. The police have detained two suspects, and the investigation is ongoing. This incident has raised serious questions about the safety of foreign tourists in India. Will justice be served swiftly in this case?
