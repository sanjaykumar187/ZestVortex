Holi 2025: Preparations for the Festival of Colors Begin!

Preparations for Holi 2025 are in full swing across India, with people gearing up to celebrate this vibrant festival. Families are buying gifts, sweets, and colors to make the occasion special for their loved ones. Preparations for Holika Dahan, the bonfire ritual, have also started, with communities organizing events in various cities. This year, many are opting for organic colors, showing increased environmental awareness. Special events for Holi have been announced in cities like Delhi and Mathura, attracting both locals and tourists. How will you celebrate this festival of colors with joy and love?
